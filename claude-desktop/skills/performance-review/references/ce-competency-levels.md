# Consulting Engineering Competency Levels Reference

## Level Definitions Overview

| Level | Summary |
|-------|---------|
| CE | Delivers customer solutions with guidance. Builds foundational expertise, supports team initiatives, develops customer-facing skills. |
| Senior CE | Leads complex customer engagements independently. Mentors others, drives strategic initiatives, recognized expertise in domain. |

---

## Technical Excellence

### CE
- Troubleshoot customer issues independently, analyzing symptoms and patterns
- Apply Jamf product expertise to solve customer problems
- Build proof-of-concept solutions that demonstrate value
- Develop scripting/automation skills (Bash, Python, Swift as appropriate)
- Document solutions clearly for customer handoff

### Senior CE
- Solve complex multi-product challenges using advanced techniques
- Design and build production-ready tools and integrations
- Lead technical architecture decisions for customer solutions
- Create reusable frameworks, scripts, and tools for the team
- Recognized as subject matter expert in specialty area
- Drive adoption of new technologies and approaches

---

## Customer Engagement

### CE
- Build rapport with customer technical teams
- Translate customer needs into technical requirements
- Communicate technical concepts clearly to varying audiences
- Follow up consistently on customer commitments
- Escalate appropriately when blocked

### Senior CE
- Establish trusted advisor relationships with customer leadership
- Identify upsell/expansion opportunities through technical discussions
- Navigate complex customer organizations and politics
- Lead customer-facing presentations and workshops
- Manage customer expectations proactively

---

## Domain Knowledge

### CE
- Build foundational knowledge in Jamf product suite
- Develop expertise in one or more verticals (Healthcare, Retail, K-12, etc.)
- Understand customer environments (MDM, identity, security stack)
- Stay current on Apple platform capabilities

### Senior CE
- Deep expertise in specific domain (Industry Solutions, Apple Technologies, Partner Integrations)
- Recognized externally as domain expert (partner community, conferences)
- Anticipate industry trends and prepare solutions proactively
- Educate team and broader organization on domain developments

---

## Solution Design

### CE
- Implement solutions balancing customer constraints and best practices
- Consider short-term and long-term customer impact
- Document technical designs for customer approval

### Senior CE
- Architect complex solutions spanning multiple products and partners
- Evaluate trade-offs and document recommendations
- Create reference architectures reusable across customers
- Balance innovation with practical customer constraints

---

## Project Execution

### CE
- Deliver customer projects on time with quality
- Communicate status and blockers proactively
- Manage multiple customer engagements concurrently

### Senior CE
- Lead complex multi-phase customer projects
- Coordinate across internal teams (Sales, Support, Product)
- Identify risks early and implement mitigations
- Model best practices for project delivery

---

## Partner & Internal Collaboration

### CE
- Collaborate effectively with Sales, Support, Product teams
- Build relationships with technology partners
- Contribute to team knowledge sharing

### Senior CE
- Drive cross-functional initiatives (SADP, Concepts, etc.)
- Establish strategic partner relationships
- Mentor and develop other team members
- Influence product roadmap through customer insights

---

## Innovation & Contribution

### CE
- Contribute to internal tools and documentation
- Participate in hackathons and innovation initiatives
- Share learnings with team

### Senior CE
- Lead development of Jamf Concepts projects
- Drive patent applications for novel solutions
- Present at conferences and community events
- Shape team strategy and direction
